Safety Alerts

Safety on construction sites is of paramount importance. It is vital that our workers arrive safe and make it home safe. Here at Moore Construction we make it our priority that our employees all make it home safe to their families. Below are the safety risks or near misses we have had to date.

Date Type of Alert Description
13-11-2023 Fire Alert Evacuation needed due to fire. Fire was caused due to a faulty wire installed. Luckily due to our evacuation rehearsals we were able to extract all personnel without any injuries.
25-01-2024 Equipment Malfunction Unsafe equipment detected and isolated. The faulty equipment was identified by one of our safety members who brought it to the attention of the work crew. The equipment was then disposed of safely.
01-02-2014 Fall Hazard Secure the perimeter for worker safety. It is important that we clean as we go in the work area to avoid any accidents that can be easily prevented.
